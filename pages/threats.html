Ransomware Surge

This week's threat is ransomware a type of malware that locks your files and demands payment to unlock them.

What Should You Do?

  • Never click on suspicious email attachments.
  • Back up your data regularly.
  • Keep your antivirus software updated.
  • Don't pay the ransom report the incident instead.
